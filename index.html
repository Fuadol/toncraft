The $TCRAFT token is built around the idea of one of the most world’s popular video game, Minecraft. Mining and building with TON is all what TONCRAFT is about, coupled with earning some $TCT alongside the adventure.

Toncraft would be launching initially on gaspump with a 13% buy from the team. Gaspump contributors and top holders before launch on dedust will be rewarded with a Toncraft pass NFT which will have a supply of 155. 50 will be allocated for rewards, 100 will be raffled out and 5 for the team. These pass NFT will enable users to participate in Project raffles, comic challenges and leaderboard and will also be a VIP pass to our upcoming TONCRAFT mobile game coming in the future.

Seasonal token burns will be done to slowly reduce the availability of the $TCT token. A certain percentage of the supply will be allocated for rewards and incentives to weekly challenges and gaming/comic tasks that will be introduced to reward players and the community

MISSION AND AIM

AIM
TonCraft aims to create a vibrant community around the TON blockchain, attracting new users through the familiar and beloved world of Minecraft. By utilizing the connection of memes, NFTs, and DeFi, Toncraft seeks to make TON blockchain accessible, fun, engaging and rewarding for everyone.
MISSION
To provide a platform for gamers, artists and Chad degens to monetize via Toncraft tokens
Integrating Toncraft tokens into a fun and engaging in-game or metaverse experience inspired by Minecraft
Making TON technology accessible and fun through a familiar and well-loved gaming theme
